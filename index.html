About Me

I'm a senior at Brown University, double concentrating in Computer Science and Music. I am a researcher in the Humans to Robots Lab (H2R) and Intelligent Robots Lab (IRL) at Brown, under research advisors: Professors George Konidaris and Stefanie Tellex. Broadly, I am interested in the intersection of robotics+natural language processing, and robotics+human-robot interaction for robots to be integrated into society to help humans with everyday tasks!
